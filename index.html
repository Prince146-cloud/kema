About Kema Skills Hub

Kema Skills Hub Uganda is a premier vocational training institution located on Ruby Haven Lane, Mawanda Road in Kampala, dedicated to empowering individuals with practical, market-ready skills that transform lives and create opportunities.

We specialize in four core areas: Fashion Design, Event Decorating, Makeup & Veiling, and Professional Baking. Our comprehensive training programs combine traditional craftsmanship with modern techniques to ensure our graduates stand out in their chosen fields.

With state-of-the-art facilities, experienced instructors, and a commitment to excellence, we provide hands-on training that prepares you for immediate employment or entrepreneurship in Uganda's growing creative and hospitality industries.

Vision

To establish a global reputation for success in a rapid changing industry and make a positive impact on society.

Mission

To cultivate a new generation for job creators who can drive innovation sustainability and creativity in the industry.
